Pasulj recipe

Description

Bean stew, slow-cooked with smoked meat and spices, perfect for warming up on cold days.

Ingridients

  • 300g dried beans, soaked overnight
  • 200g smoked meat, ribs or sausage
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • salt, pepper, bay leaf

Steps

  1. Boil beans, discard the water, then cook with smoked meat in fresh water
  2. In pan, fry onions add paprika powder and flour
  3. Simmer until tender, season more if needed
