Trilece

Description

Trilece is a soft and spongy cake soaked in a blend of three types of milk, topped with a layer of caramel.

Ingridients

  • 4 eggs
  • 100g sugar
  • 120g flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 400ml whole milk
  • 200ml condensed milk
  • 200ml evaporated milk
  • caramel syrup

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs and sugar until fluffy, fold in flour and baking powder
  2. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius for 25 minutes
  3. Mix the three milks and pour over the cooled cake
  4. Add caramel on top, and chill
